Journey

ITK began its journey in 2021 as a visionary venture of Narayan Jangid, driven by a passion for robotics and a desire to make a lasting impact in the field. With a clear vision in mind, Narayan embarked on a mission to create innovative robotic solutions that would transform industries and revolutionize the way we interact with technology. In 2023, ITK reached a significant milestone as a team of 5-7 talented individuals joined forces, bringing together diverse expertise in engineering, design, and robotics. With a shared enthusiasm for pushing the boundaries of technological innovation, the team at ITK set out to develop a groundbreaking product called Robonari.

Robonari, our flagship product, was designed to bridge the gap between humans and machines, redefining the way brands connect with their target audience. From its inception, our team worked tirelessly to ensure that Robonari possessed not only advanced robotic capabilities but also the ability to engage in natural conversations, review products, and deliver compelling presentations. As our company continued to grow, we recognized the importance of fostering talent and providing opportunities for young minds to contribute to our vision. In line with this belief, we actively sought out interns who shared our passion for robotics and offered them valuable hands-on experience within our dynamic team. These interns became an integral part of our journey, bringing fresh perspectives and innovative ideas to the table.
